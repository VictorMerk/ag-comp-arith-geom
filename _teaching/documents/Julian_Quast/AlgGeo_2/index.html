Course Description

This course continues the study of algebraic geometry, building on the foundations established in Algebraic Geometry 1. Topics include advanced concepts in scheme theory, cohomology, and modern techniques in algebraic geometry.

Topics Covered

  • Scheme theory and morphisms
  • Cohomology of sheaves
  • Serre duality
  • Riemann-Roch theorem
  • Moduli spaces
  • Deformation theory
  • Applications to number theory
