Course Description

This course provides an introduction to abstract algebra, covering fundamental concepts in group theory, ring theory, and field theory. The course serves as a foundation for advanced studies in algebra and number theory.

Topics Covered

  • Groups: definitions, subgroups, homomorphisms, quotient groups
  • Rings: definitions, ideals, ring homomorphisms, quotient rings
  • Fields: field extensions, finite fields
  • Polynomial rings and factorization
  • Basic Galois theory
